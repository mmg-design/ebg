Our Story

Built to serve.
Built to last.

Exact Benefits Group was founded on a straightforward premise: that individuals, families, and employers deserve clear, accurate, and unbiased guidance when making benefits decisions — decisions that carry real consequences for health, finances, and security.

What began as a focused Medicare advisory practice has grown into a multi-division benefits organization. Today, EBG oversees two distinct tracks: Exact Medicare, serving individuals and families navigating government-sponsored coverage, and our employer and partner solutions division, supporting organizations and benefit brokers with compliant, scalable advisory services.

At the organizational level, Exact Benefits Group maintains rigorous governance standards that govern both divisions. Our commitment to regulatory compliance, fiduciary responsibility, and long-term client stewardship isn't a selling point — it's the operating standard from which everything else follows.

Our governance commitment

Exact Benefits Group operates under defined governance standards that guide both divisions. Leadership is held accountable to the same principles governing our client relationships: transparency, accuracy, and long-term thinking.
